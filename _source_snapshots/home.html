Index Building Group — your trusted local builder serving Canberra and surrounding regions.

Built on Trust. Focused on You.

We’re a construction company that puts people first.
Founded by Stephen Zhou, a licensed builder with over a decade of hands on experience, Index Building Group was created with one goal: to make construction feel simple, transparent, and personal. Since starting his building career in 2010, Stephen has proudly delivered high quality projects for families and businesses across the ACT and beyond, always with a hands on approach and a commitment to doing things right.
We understand that construction can feel overwhelming, so we make it easy. We hold your hand through the entire process, from design and approvals to final handover, ensuring your ideas are heard, your budget respected, and your project delivered on time.
Whether you're renovating your family home, upgrading a commercial space, or starting fresh with a new build, we’re here to guide you every step of the way. we bring structure, clarity, and direction to your building journey so you always know what’s next and never feel left in the dark.
